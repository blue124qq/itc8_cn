ALL ABOUT THE SOLUTION

In Control over Your Cows 24/7

Monitor every cow on your dairy farm 24/7 with CowManager. With real-time insights into the fertility, health, nutrition, and location of your cows, you can focus on the right cow at the right time. The clear data helps you to work more efficiently, save medical expenses and increase the general health and productivity of your cows. Always be in full control of your herd with CowManager.

Simplicity - The ear sensor

Simplicity -
The ear sensor

The revolutionary ear sensor technology provides accurate information about your herd’s fertility, health, nutrition and location. The sensor fits around an RFID tag.
Read more...

Accurate - The data

Accurate -
The data

CowManager is a complete plug-and-play system that is compatible with your farm management system. The data transmitted by the sensors is collected by routers. These routers can be installed in barns or feed lots. For pure grazing farms routers are charged by solar chargers in the field. A coordinating device connected to your computer ensures optimal data flow.

Real-time -
Smartphone or computer

CowManager monitors your herd 24/7 in any situation, regardless of farm size. It’s the ultimate tool for continuously monitoring your cows’ fertility, health, nutrition and location, receiving real-time information on your smartphone and/or other devices.
