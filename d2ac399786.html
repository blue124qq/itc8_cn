Kees van Dooren

Reporter Boerderij

Kees van Dooren has worked since March 2007 at Boerderij (a Dutch language publication), a sister publication of All About Feed, Pig Progress, Poultry World and Dairy Global. In his first year he held the position of reporter for the pig markets section. Since 2008 he has been writing about animal husbandry.
