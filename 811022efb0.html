Future proof solutions in 4 core application areas

Feed additives are an effective solution to Control Pathogens, fortify animal Body Defence systems, and improve Intestinal Health & Nutrient Efficiency, aiming to leave the era of antibiotic usage behind.

FRAmelco has over 40 years of experience in developing feed additive concepts improving animal health and performance. In close collaboration with renowned international universities and research facilities, FRAmelco develops innovative feed additive solutions to overcome worldwide challenges in livestock production.
